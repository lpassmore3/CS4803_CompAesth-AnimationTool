CS 4803 - Computational Aesthetics
Project 3: Animation
Authors: Austin Passmore & Seunghwan Lee

To play our emotion animations:
	- Press Q to toggle off the menu
	- Press Y to toggle on our saved animations
	- Press 1(discouraged), 2(happy), or 3(scared) to select our animations
	- Press L to toggle off the keyframes and curves
	- Press A to play an animation after one has been selected

Feel free to toggle the menu back on and play around with the program after
watching our animations.
